[1] The Wye River Accord provided for an airport at Gaza , [2] giving Palestinians their own airport with Israel in charge of security . [3] Gaza International Airport opened on Nov. 24 , 1998 with arrival of an Egypt Air flight , [4] but the highlight was landing of the first Palestinian Airlines plane , [5] greeted joyously by a celebrating crowd . [6] Yasser Arafat was on hand to greet these and later flights . [7] The airport was expected to provide stimulus to the Palestinian economy as well as facilitating Palestinians ' travel abroad .
