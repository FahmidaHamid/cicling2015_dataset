[1] On Oct. 16 , 1998 British police arrested former Chilean dictator Pinochet on a Spanish warrant [2] charging murder of Spaniards in Chile , 1973-1983 . [3] Fidel Castro denounced the arrest . [4] The Chilean government protested strongly . [5] While the British government defended the arrest , [6] it and the Spanish government took no stand on extradition of Pinochet to Spain , leaving it to the courts . [7] Chilean legislators lobbied in Madrid against extradition , while others endorsed it . [8] Then new charges were filed for crimes against Swiss and French citizens .
