[1] On the eve of the 10th anniversary of the 1988 downing of Pan Am 103 over Lockerbie , Scotland , there is hope [2] that the Libya suspects might soon be brought to trial . [3] UN General Secretary Kofi Annan has met with Libyan officials [4] and an agreement in principle has been reached on the need for a trial . [5] Libya seeks assurances [6] that the trial will not be politicized . [7] Libya has been under a UN flight ban since 1992 in an effort [8] to get them to turn over the suspected bombers for trial by Scottish authorities . [9] The citizens of Lockerbie marked the anniversary of the bombing with a memorial service .
