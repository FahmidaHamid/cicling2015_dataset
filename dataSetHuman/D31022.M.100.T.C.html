[1] At least 60 teenagers were killed [2] and another 160 were injured in a dance hall fire in Goteborg , Sweden , Sweden 's second largest city . [7] The dance attendees were mostly immigrant children from representing 19 nationalities , [8] including Somalia , Ethiopia , Iraq , Iran and former Yugoslavia .
