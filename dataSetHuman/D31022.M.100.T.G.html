[1] Sweden 's deadliest fire occurred on Halloween [2] when a dance hall in Goteborg , [3] filled with teenagers , burst into flames . [4] The cause is not known , [5] but the hall , approved for 150 , [6] contained as many as 400 and one of the two exits was blocked . [7] Sixty were killed and between 150 and 173 injured . [8] Most were immigrants or their children [9] and represented 19 nationalities . [10] Many felt the rescue was late . [11] Rescuers said they had trouble understanding the first call , [12] but were there in 6 minutes . [13] Condolences came from Swedish authorities .
