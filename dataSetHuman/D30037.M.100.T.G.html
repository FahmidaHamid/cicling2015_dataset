[1] Ariel Sharon was named Israeli foreign minister just weeks before a scheduled peace summit in the US , [2] aimed at negotiating an Israeli withdrawal from 13 % of the West Bank . [3] Arabs reacted strongly [4] and said the hard-line , right-wing , warrior and former defense minister would kill any progress for a settlement in the mid-East . [5] Many blame Sharon for a 1982 massacre of Palestinian civilians at refugee camps in Lebanon . [6] Others believed his appointment was an effort to placate the far right [7] and gain their support for the Netanyahu government and the concessions necessary for the peace process . [8] Sharon vowed not to shake Yasser Arafat 's hand .
