[1] As the U.S. government pressed its antitrust case against Microsoft Corp. in November 1998 , America Online ( AOL ) [2] proposed an alliance with Netscape Communications and Sun Microsystems . [3] The three-pronged deal promised to provide on-line services , Internet software and electronic commerce . [4] AOL was to buy Netscape [5] and forge a partnership with Sun , [6] benefiting all three [7] and giving technological independence from Microsoft . [8] Microsoft lawyers argued unconvincingly that AOL 's purchase of Netscape would undermine the government 's antitrust case , [9] based in large part on Netscape 's complaint .
