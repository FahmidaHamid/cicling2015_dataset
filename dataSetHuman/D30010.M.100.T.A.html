[1] On Nov. 6 , 1998 a suicide auto-bomb attack on a Jerusalem market killed two Palestinian `` martyrs '' and wounded 21 Israelis . [2] Israel 's cabinet immediately suspended consideration of the Wye peace agreement and the Prime Minister vowed to expand Jewish settlement in the Arab sector of Jerusalem . [3] Islamic Jihad claimed responsibility for the bombing [4] and identified the Wye accord as its target . [5] Israel called for outlawing Islamic Jihad and Hamas [6] while the Palestinians accused Israel as using the bombing as a pretext for delaying implementation of Wye . [7] The `` martyrs '' , 21 and 24 , were both alumni of Israeli jails .
