[1] The Wye River accord has not been implemented . [2] As the Israeli cabinet was considering the agreement , Islamic Jihad militants exploded a car bomb in nearby Mahane Yehuda market . [3] The cabinet suspended ratification of the agreement , [4] demanding the Palestinian Authority take steps against terrorism .
