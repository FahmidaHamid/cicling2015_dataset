[1] The Truth and Reconciliation Commission , [2] which was established [3] to look into the human rights violations [4] committed during the long struggle against white rule , [5] released its final report . [6] In what has been described as one of the most complete reports of its kind , the commission blames most of the atrocities on the former South African government . [7] The ANC also came under fire for committing some atrocities during the struggle . [8] Nelson Mandella 's ex-wife , Winnie could be prosecuted for the part [9] she played in such violations . [10] Missing from the report was De Klerk , [11] who threatened to sue [12] if he was mentioned in connection with the atrocities .
