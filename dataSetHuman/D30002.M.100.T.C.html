[1] Hurricane Mitch , category 5 hurricane , brought widespread death and destruction to Central American . [2] Especially hard hit was Honduras [3] where an estimated 6,076 people lost their lives . [4] The hurricane , which lingered off the coast of Honduras for 3 days before moving off , [5] flooded large areas , [6] destroying crops and property . [7] The U.S. and European Union were joined by Pope John Paul II in a call for money and workers [8] to help the stricken area . [9] President Clinton sent Tipper Gore , wife of Vice President Gore to the area [10] to deliver much needed supplies to the area , [11] demonstrating U.S. commitment to the recovery of the region .
