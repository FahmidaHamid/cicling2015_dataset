[1] As impeachment proceedings in the House approached a climax , House members were stunned by an admission [2] from House Speaker elect Livingston [3] that he was guilty of an adulterous affair . [4] The admission follows disclosures by Hustler published Larry Flynn , [5] who had offered one million dollars to anyone who could provide such information on a House or Senate member . [6] Livingston 's admission was met with a standing ovation by House members , [7] as was his subsequent resignation . [8] In resigning , Livingston called on President Clinton [9] to do the same . [10] Political pundits see the whole process as driven by partisan politics .
