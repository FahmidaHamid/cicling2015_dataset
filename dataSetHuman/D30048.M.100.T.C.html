[1] As Latin American nations gathered for the Ibero-American summit , there was general concern for the global economy . [2] Most leaders attending warned [3] that the downturn in the global economy could have dire consequences . [4] The crisis facing Latin America has had an effect on the U.S. economy [5] as exports declined as a result of the recession . [6] Brazil 's economy was especially hard hit [7] and it has entered into talks with the IMF [8] to secure loans needed to bolster its economy . [9] The outcome of these talks could depend on the outcome of the Brazilian elections . [10] In an effort to cuts its deficit , Brazil has cut back on the perks of government workers .
