[1] The government suit against Microsoft is the most aggressive antitrust case in a quarter century . [2] The heart of the case is the Internet browser battle between Microsoft and Netscape . [3] Microsoft says [4] that its Internet Explorer is an integral part of its Windows system , the industry dominant operating system . [5] Microsoft , [6] it is argued , [7] has told computer manufacturers [8] that if they want Windows , [9] they must forgo Netscape . [10] The Justice Department and 20 states are joined in the action [11] brought under the Sherman Antitrust Act . [12] Microsoft 's chairman , Bill Gates , [13] usually seen as a visionary is portrayed in much darker tones in the trial .
