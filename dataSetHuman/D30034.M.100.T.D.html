[1] The future of the former Portuguese island colony East Timor is in doubt . [2] Indonesia invaded it in 1975 [3] and later annexed it , [4] but not recognized by the UN . [5] East Timor 's spiritual leader , Bishop Carlos Belok the 1996 Nobel Peace laureate , has reported killings by Indonesian troops . [6] Portugal has accused Indonesia of failing to reduce its military presence in East Timor . [7] Australia and Taiwan are reluctant to antagonize Indonesia on this issue . [8] Violence has also occurred in Jakarta and west Timor .
