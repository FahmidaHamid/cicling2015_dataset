[1] A category 5 storm , Hurricane Mitch roared across the northwest Caribbean with 180 mph winds across a 350-mile front [2] that devastated the mainland and islands of Central America . [3] Although the force of the storm diminished , at least 8,000 people died from wind , waves and flood damage . [4] The greatest losses were in Honduras [5] where some 6,076 people perished . [6] Around 2,000 people were killed in Nicaragua , 239 in El Salvador , 194 in Guatemala , seven in Costa Rica and six in Mexico . [7] At least 569,000 people were homeless across Central America .
