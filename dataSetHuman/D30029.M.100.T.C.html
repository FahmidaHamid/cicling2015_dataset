[1] Gale-force winds and high seas struck the 155 participants in Australia 's Sydney to Hobart yacht race . [2] One sailor , Glyn Charles , [3] who was swept off the yacht Sword of Orion , [4] is presumed drowned [5] and two seamen on the Business Post Naiad were killed . [6] Search and rescue efforts were stretched to the limit also [7] as three yachts turned up missing . [8] One yacht , the B-52 was found with the crew safe . [9] Race organizers never considered canceling the race , [10] claiming that the skippers controlled whether to stay in the race or drop out . [11] A total of 37 yachts were forced out of the race , [12] many of which had lost their masts .
