[1] A fire at an overcrowded dance hall in Goteborg , Sweden killed 60 [2] and injured 180 . [3] Most victims were 13-18 , immigrants [4] covering 19 nationalities . [5] The 2-story brick building was rated for 150 [6] but held 400 . [7] Owned by the Macedonian Association , [8] it had been rented out to 8 party arrangers for a Halloween dance , [9] held on the 2nd floor . [10] One of two exit stairways was blocked by fire . [11] A panicky phone call in poor Swedish to authorities took 3 minutes to understand . [12] Fire trucks were on the scene 6 minutes later . [13] The explosive , fast-spreading fire reached 600C .
