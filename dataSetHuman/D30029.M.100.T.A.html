[1] As contestants prepared for the 1998 running of Australia 's 630 nautical mile Sydney-to-Hobart yacht race , the American maxi yacht Sayonara emerged [2] as the favorite followed by Australia 's Brindabella and Wild Thing . [3] Winds of 15-30 knots were forecast . [4] The first day of the race Brindabella and Sayonara led . [5] By the end of the second day horrendous conditions had developed with gales reaching 80 knots and waves as high as 10 meters . [6] By dusk of the third day three crewmen were dead , three missing , many injured and about half of the 115 yachts were forced out of the race . [7] Sayonara was narrowly ahead of Brindabella and expected to reach Hobart
