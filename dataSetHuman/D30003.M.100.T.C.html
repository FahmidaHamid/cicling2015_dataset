[1] Former Chilean dictator Augusto Pinochet has been arrested in London at the request of the Spanish government . [2] Pinochet , in London for back surgery , was arrested in his hospital room . [3] Spain is seeking extradition of Pinochet from London to Spain [4] to face charges of murder in the deaths of Spanish citizens in Chile under Pinochet 's rule in the 1970s and 80s . [5] The arrest raised confusion in the international community [6] as the legality of the move is debated . [7] Pinochet supporters say that Pinochet 's arrest is illegal , [8] claiming he has diplomatic immunity . [9] The final outcome of the extradition request lies with the Spanish courts .
