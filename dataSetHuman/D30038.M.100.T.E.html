[1] IOC executive board member , Marc Hodler , alleged [2] that agents from cities bidding to host Olympic Games offer IOC members bribes [3] to favor their city . [4] The allegation was made public [5] when Holder revealed [6] the Salt Lake City 2002 Games [7] organizers offered scholarship payments to IOC members ' relatives . [8] The IOC is investigating the charges and further allegations of Olympic corruption in voting for the games of 1996 , 1998 and 2000 . [9] IOC President Samaranch called for an investigation of the allegations , [10] promising to expel anyone found guilty , [11] and ordered Hodler to keep quiet . [12] Japan denied allegations of bribery to host the 1998 Winter Games .
